World Happiness 2019

The world happiness report was created as an effort by the United Nations to give importance to happiness and wellbeing. Most of it's data are provided by the Gallup Global Emotions report which creates a happiness score ("Happiness Ladder") within a range of 0 to 10, with 10 being the happiest based on economic and social variables. The world map shows the Happiness score of 156 in 2019.
